Frequently asked questions

Answers after a traffic accident

What does instructing you cost me?

In a no-fault traffic accident, the other party's liability insurer regularly covers the necessary legal fees. No legal expenses insurance is required for this.

Do I need legal expenses insurance?

No. In a no-fault accident the other party's insurer usually covers the costs. If you are at fault, legal expenses insurance helps – we clarify this with you in advance.

May I choose my own expert?

Yes. In a no-fault accident you have the right to your own independent expert – not the one from the other party's insurer.

How quickly will I get a response?

Usually within 24 hours. The fastest way to reach us is via WhatsApp.

Do you also represent nationwide?

Yes. The process is digital, so we can represent you throughout Germany.
