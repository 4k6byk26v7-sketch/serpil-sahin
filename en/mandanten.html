Immediate checklist

The most important steps at the accident scene

  • 1Secure the accident scene. Hazard lights on, set up the warning triangle, wear a safety vest.
  • 2Attend to the injured. In case of injury, immediately call 112.
  • 3Call the police (110). Especially for major damage, disputes or personal injury.
  • 4Document everything. Photos of the vehicles, damage, licence plates, final positions and surroundings.
  • 5Exchange details. Names, addresses, licence plates, insurance – note down witnesses.
  • 6Do not sign anything. Do not admit fault – speak to us first.
  • 7Contact us. We take over from here and secure your claims.

Good to know

After a non-fault accident you are entitled to your own independent assessor and your own lawyer – the costs are usually borne by the opposing insurer.
