Traffic law – our focus

From minor damage to personal injury: we know the insurers' tactics and recover what you are entitled to.

Accident claims settlement

We handle all correspondence with the opposing insurer and make sure every item – repairs, diminished value, loss of use, towing costs – is reimbursed correctly.

Pain compensation & personal injury

In case of injuries we pursue appropriate pain compensation, loss of earnings, household assistance damages and treatment costs – including late effects.

Fines & administrative offences

We check fine notices for measurement and formal errors and file objections in time. Goal: dismissal or reduced sanctions.

Driving bans & points

Facing a driving ban or licence revocation? We defend your mobility – from hardship arguments to court proceedings.

Traffic criminal law

For allegations such as drunk driving, hit-and-run or coercion in traffic, we act as your defence counsel.

Vehicle purchase law

Vehicle defects, rescission of purchase or disputes with garages and dealers – here too we represent your interests.
